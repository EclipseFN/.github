## üöÄ How to Play

To join the server and start a match, you need to download our launcher.

1.  Join our **[Discord](https://discord.gg/eclipsefn)**.
2.  Go to the `#download` channel.
3.  Download the **EclipseFN Launcher**.
4.  Launch the application and log in with your Discord account.
5.  Download the game via the launcher.
6.  Click on **Launch** and you are ready to play!

> **Note:** In case of issues, a complete tutorial is available on our Discord `#tutorial`.
